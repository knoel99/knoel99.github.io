Geology - Big Data enables the discovery of new rare minerals

  • Big Data is now being used by geologists to discover new rare minerals.
  • Analytical technologies enable prediction of the existence and location of unknown minerals.
  • This approach is compared to Amazon and Netflix's recommendation systems.
  • Minerals form in networks, similar to interactions on social media platforms.
  • Over 5,200 mineral species have been catalogued with detailed information.
  • Databases contain information on mineral composition, properties, and locations.
  • Big Data allows for systematic discovery of new deposits, previously based on chance.
  • Ten rare minerals have already been discovered using this method.
  • Scientists plan to use these techniques to explore the geological history of Mars and the Moon.
