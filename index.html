About the Program

Data is now a key asset for companies. Actors of the energy transition also deal with large volumes of various data from different sources, much of which is georeferenced. This program trains data managers specialized in managing data related to the energy mix.

By the end of this program, you will be able to:

  • Understand the acquisition, processing, and interpretation of geographical data.
  • Apply best practices in data management for georesources and energy projects.
  • Quickly integrate into industrial workgroups.

International Certifications

  • Certified Data Management Professional (CDMP) by DAMA.
  • Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM®).

Instructors

All courses are delivered by ENSG Géomatique and IFP School professors, as well as recognized professionals in the field.

Why is this program important?

Managing geospatial data is crucial in projects related to georesources and the energy mix. Optimized data management allows for better value extraction and helps understand the impact of industrial projects on the environment.
