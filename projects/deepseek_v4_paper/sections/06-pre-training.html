Alternative Reading Angle: Pre-Training

Commentary

This section is the engineering backbone of the entire paper — where architectural ambition meets the messy reality of training trillion-parameter MoE models. The core tension is efficiency vs. capability: DeepSeek-V4-Flash must outperform the larger V3.2 with fewer active parameters, while V4-Pro pushes the frontier by scaling everything up. The 32T-token corpus, with its deliberate emphasis on long documents and agentic coding data, signals that the team isn't just scaling compute — they're reshaping the data diet to match where they want the model to excel.

The most revealing details are the instability mitigation techniques. Anticipatory Routing is essentially a temporal hack: use stale parameters for routing decisions to break the feedback loop where outlier activations reinforce themselves through expert selection. It's a pragmatic admission that MoE routing creates a vicious cycle the optimizer can't self-correct. SwiGLU Clamping — capping values to [−10, 10] — is even more blunt, yet both work without performance degradation. The authors openly acknowledge they lack theoretical justification, which is refreshingly honest and suggests the community still doesn't fully understand MoE training dynamics at this scale.

The training schedule itself tells a story: start dense, go sparse only at 64K context, and extend to 1M gradually. This phased approach reflects a constraint — sparse attention mechanisms need a mature representation space before they can safely replace full attention without collapsing.

Why Muon + AdamW hybrid?

The Muon optimizer (momentum-based, with orthogonalized updates) handles the bulk of parameters more efficiently than Adam, but the authors fall back to AdamW for embeddings, prediction heads, and RMSNorm — modules where update orthogonality might disrupt delicate scaling. The RMS rescaling to 0.18 is specifically chosen so Muon's update magnitude matches AdamW's, allowing a shared learning rate schedule. This is a practical engineering choice, not a theoretical one.
